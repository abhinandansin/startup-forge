ABOUT STARTUP FORGE

Startup Forge 1.0 is an innovative entrepreneurial competition designed to ignite the founder spirit in the next generation. It's a dynamic platform where aspiring entrepreneurs pitch their groundbreaking ideas, receive mentor feedback, and compete in a series of challenging rounds.

This three-round competition tests participants on:

  • HEAT ROUND (26 March) - Rigorous idea screening based on originality, feasibility, and market potential
  • HAMMER ROUND (27 March) - Structured PowerPoint presentations followed by intense Q&A sessions with industry experts
  • FORGE ROUND (28 March) - Real-time business challenges and strategic pivoting under pressure

Whether you're a visionary student with a game-changing idea or a young entrepreneur looking to scale your startup, Startup Forge 1.0 is your arena to compete, learn, and shine. Join us in this exciting journey of innovation and growth!
